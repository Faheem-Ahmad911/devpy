Roadmap for Beginners in Data Science

Basics

  • Learn statistics, probability, and linear algebra.
  • Master Python or R.

Tools

  • Libraries: Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow.
  • Visualization: Matplotlib, Seaborn, Tableau.

Experience

  • Work on projects and join Kaggle competitions.
  • Gain internships or freelance opportunities.

  • Communicate insights clearly.
  • Strengthen problem-solving and critical thinking.

Growth

  • Stay updated with tools, AI models, and policies.
